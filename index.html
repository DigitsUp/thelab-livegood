The Opportunity

LiveGood Has the Audience.
Now Let's Monetize It.

LiveGood's model is built around a passionate, health-driven community with high repeat purchase potential. Most of that lifetime value is sitting dormant in the list — recoverable through the right automation architecture and a consistent campaign cadence.

01

Abandoned Revenue Recovery

Cart, checkout, and browse abandonment are the highest-ROI flows in retention. Without a complete abandonment suite, LiveGood is leaving meaningful first-party revenue on the table every single day.

02

Welcome Experience & First Purchase

The popup subscriber who never converts is a common leak point. A segmented welcome series — built around LiveGood's product categories and health goals — drives that first purchase before interest fades.

03

Post-Purchase LTV Expansion

LiveGood's catalog is deep — vitamins, CBD, greens, protein, skincare, bundles. A structured post-purchase flow cross-sells related products, educates on benefits, and builds the habit loop that drives subscription and repeat orders.

04

Win-Back & Sunset

Lapsed members and dormant subscribers are high-intent when re-engaged correctly. A proper winback sequence — with a sunset to protect deliverability — keeps the list clean and the engaged segment growing.

Cart AbandonmentTriggered: add-to-cart, no checkout initiated

Phase 1
Checkout AbandonmentTriggered: checkout started, no purchase
Highest-converting abandonment flow — these buyers got furthest down the funnel. 2-email sequence, urgency-forward, reinforcing the member value proposition and 60-day guarantee.
Phase 1
Welcome SeriesTriggered: popup subscribe, no prior purchase
A brand-first, product-second welcome that earns trust before it asks for a sale. Introduces LiveGood's mission, member pricing advantage, and top-selling product categories. 4–5 emails over 10 days, with purchase behavior exits.
Phase 1
Post-Purchase — New CustomerTriggered: first-ever order placed
Celebrates the first purchase, confirms member status, and begins planting the seeds for order #2. Usage tips, complement products, a membership upgrade prompt if not already a member.
Phase 1
Post-Purchase — Repeat CustomerTriggered: 2nd purchase onward
Deepens the relationship. Recognizes loyalty, introduces adjacent catalog products based on what they've bought, reinforces the subscription/auto-ship value with a direct nudge.
Phase 2
Win-BackTriggered: no purchase in 90–120 days
Reactivates lapsed customers with a compelling re-engagement sequence. Product re-introduction, "what's new," and a timed offer. 3 emails with an SMS touchpoint on the final send.
Phase 2
SunsetTriggered: no engagement in 180+ days
A clean, honest last-chance flow that either re-activates or suppresses — keeping the list healthy and deliverability protected. One final offer, then a graceful exit from the active list.

Campaign Strategy

Consistent Sends.
Smarter Segmentation.

Two service tiers designed to match LiveGood's growth stage and ambitions. Every campaign is segmented, scheduled, and built with LiveGood's health-product catalog and member audience at the center.

Tier 1 / Foundation

1–2 Campaigns / Week

4–8 / month

The right baseline — consistent sends to a clean, segmented list. Every campaign splits at minimum between new and return customer audiences.

  • New vs. return customer split on every send
  • Engagement-tiered suppression built in
  • Monthly campaign calendar approved by 25th
  • Subject line + preview text for every send
  • Monthly performance report
Tier 2 / Growth

3–5 Campaigns / Week

12–20 / month

For brands ready to scale. Higher send frequency with full segmented split testing across audience cohorts, product lines, and engagement tiers.

  • Everything in Tier 1
  • 3–5 targeted segments per send (health goal, product line, purchase history)
  • A/B testing on subject lines, CTAs, and send times
  • SMS campaign sends alongside email cadence
  • Bi-weekly strategy check-in calls
  • Engagement re-warming sequences between major sends
Segmentation philosophy: LiveGood's catalog spans vastly different health categories — weight management, brain health, immune support, skincare, and more. We treat these as separate content universes. Subscribers who've bought greens and reds get a different send from someone who opened the Methylene Blue or CBD products. Purchase history drives the split; engagement data drives the timing.
